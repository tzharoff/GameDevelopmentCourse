### Week 7: Advanced Shader Graph Techniques

**Objective**: Dive deeper into Shader Graph to create complex and visually appealing shaders, enhancing the aesthetics of your game.

---

#### Monday: Complex Shader Structures
- **Morning Session**:
  - Building on basic Shader Graph knowledge.
  - Exploring complex node structures for advanced effects.
  - Introduction to procedural textures and patterns.
- **Solo Project Work**:
  - Experiment with creating complex shader structures and procedural textures.

#### Tuesday: Utilizing Lighting and Textures in Shaders
- **Morning Session**:
  - Techniques for incorporating lighting models into shaders.
  - Advanced texture manipulation using Shader Graph.
  - Creating shaders that react to game lighting.
- **Solo Project Work**:
  - Develop shaders that interact with Unity's lighting system and apply advanced texture techniques.

#### Wednesday: Shader Optimization for Game Performance
- **Morning Session**:
  - Understanding the impact of shaders on game performance.
  - Techniques for optimizing shaders.
  - Best practices for balancing visual quality and performance.
- **Solo Project Work**:
  - Optimize existing shaders for performance without compromising visual quality.

#### Thursday: Creative Shader Effects
- **Morning Session**:
  - Exploring artistic and creative uses of shaders (e.g., holographic effects, water, reflections).
  - Implementing environmental shaders (sky, terrain).
  - Tips for thinking creatively with Shader Graph.
- **Solo Project Work**:
  - Create a unique and artistic shader effect for a game environment or object.

#### Friday: Review and Advanced Shader Mini-Project
- **Morning Session**:
  - Review of advanced Shader Graph techniques.
  - Q&A session to clarify complex concepts.
  - Introduction to the mini-project: Designing an advanced shader for a specific application.
- **Solo Project Work**:
  - Design and develop an advanced shader for a specific element or scene in a game.
- **End of Day**:
  - Showcase your advanced shader in a scene or demo.
  - Group critique and feedback session with instructors and peers.

---

**Note for Students**: This week is about pushing the boundaries of what you can create with shaders. Let your creativity flow and experiment with different techniques to create unique visual effects. Remember, the most impressive shaders often come from a blend of technical skill and artistic vision. Enjoy the process of bringing your imaginative ideas to life!
